Common Questions

What is recruitment analytics software?

Recruitment analytics software collects and visualizes hiring data — time-to-hire, source effectiveness, pipeline conversion, and cost-per-hire. Most tools report on what already happened. Majhi OS goes further: it monitors mandate health in real time and executes corrective actions before searches fail.

How is Majhi OS different from recruiting analytics tools?

Standard analytics tools are lagging-indicator dashboards. They tell you a search took 90 days after it took 90 days. Majhi OS provides leading indicators — live Hiring Health Scores per mandate — and acts autonomously when scores drop. It's the difference between a speedometer and an autopilot.

What metrics does Majhi OS track?

Majhi OS tracks mandate health scores, response decay rates, pipeline stage conversion, recruiter load balance, shortlist approval rates, outreach effectiveness, time-to-qualified-candidate, and hiring velocity — all in real time, per mandate.

Can Majhi OS replace our existing ATS?

No — and it doesn't try to. Majhi OS operates above your existing ATS as the observability and execution layer. It reads signals from your current stack and acts on them. Your ATS remains your system of record; Majhi OS becomes your system of action.
