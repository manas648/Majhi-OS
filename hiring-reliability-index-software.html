HRI inside Majhi OS:
live scores, alerts, and recovery triggers.

The Hiring Reliability Index™ is not a report you generate. It is a live operational view — built into the Majhi OS platform, updated continuously, and wired to automatic recovery sequences when scores breach thresholds.

The Dashboard

One screen. Every active mandate. Live HRI.

The Majhi OS mandate dashboard shows every active search with its current HRI score, the 7-day trend, and the primary signal driving any change. No status fields to fill in. No weekly updates to chase. The system surfaces what’s happening across all mandates simultaneously.

Mandate Health Board

All active searches displayed by HRI score. At-risk mandates surface to the top automatically. Sorted by signal severity, not by alphabetical order or start date.

Signal Decomposition Panel

Click any mandate to see all five sub-signals. Which one dropped. By how much. Over what timeframe. The system shows the signal, not just the score.

Trend Visualization

7-day and 30-day HRI trend per mandate. See whether a score at 61 is stable, recovering, or declining. Trajectory matters as much as point-in-time score.

Alerts and Recovery

The system acts, not just reports.

When an HRI score breaches a threshold, Majhi OS doesn’t wait for a recruiter to notice. Alerts fire to the relevant team member. Recovery sequence recommendations surface. If the mandate is in critical territory, the system can initiate certain recovery actions autonomously.

Threshold Alerts

Configurable alert thresholds per mandate. Default: alert when HRI drops 15 points in 7 days or falls below 60. Sent to recruiting lead and, optionally, hiring manager.

Recovery Recommendations

When an alert fires, the system surfaces the specific intervention that recovered similar mandates in the same signal-drop pattern. Not generic advice — mandate-specific.

Autonomous Actions

For pre-approved recovery sequences (outreach pivot, alternative sourcing channel, sequence adjustment), Majhi OS can execute automatically without manual trigger.

Executive Visibility Layer

The CFO and CEO view.

The HRI feeds an executive visibility layer that translates mandate health into business language. Not recruiter metrics — hiring velocity, cost exposure, and pipeline risk in terms that inform a board conversation.

The HRI is the metric that moves the conversation from ‘how is the search going’ to ‘here is the exact risk and the exact action we are taking to address it.’
