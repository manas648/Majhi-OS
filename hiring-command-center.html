What Is the Hiring Command Center?

The Hiring Command Center is the operational interface of Majhi OS — the live dashboard where TA leaders, CHROs, and search directors see the real-time state of every active mandate, identify at-risk searches, and authorize or review autonomous recovery actions. It is designed to feel like a command center, not a spreadsheet.

What the Command Center Displays

The Hiring Command Center surfaces six operational data streams simultaneously:

🏥

Live Mandate Health Scores

Real-time composite health score for every active search — green, amber, or red — with the specific signals driving each score.

⚠️

Active Alerts

Mandates that have crossed health thresholds or triggered escalation conditions — surfaced prominently with recommended actions.

👥

Recruiter Utilization Map

Live view of which recruiters are operating at capacity, which have bandwidth, and whether rebalancing is recommended.

📈

Pipeline Velocity Tracker

Stage-by-stage velocity for each active mandate — showing where pipelines are moving normally and where they are slowing.

🔄

Recovery Action Queue

Pending and active recovery sequences — with status, elapsed time, and effectiveness signals for each intervention.

💰

Financial Impact Ticker

Running cost of open seats, projected time-to-fill, and daily revenue impact of each unfilled role.

The Mission Control Design Principle

Majhi OS is deliberately designed to feel like mission control — not a CRM or ATS interface. The emotional experience: 'I am monitoring a live system and I have the authority and intelligence to intervene.'

“NASA mission controllers see the state of every system simultaneously and can authorize an intervention in seconds. That is what a TA director should be able to do for their hiring system.” — Manas Majhi

Who Uses the Command Center

Three personas use the Hiring Command Center with different levels of operational authority: TA leaders and search directors (full operational access — authorize interventions, manage recruiter assignments), CHROs and VP People (strategic view — mandate health, team performance, financial impact), and CEOs (executive summary — organizational hiring health, critical at-risk searches, strategic implications).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Hiring Command Center?

The Hiring Command Center is the operational interface of Majhi OS — the live dashboard where TA leaders and CHROs see the real-time state of every active mandate, identify at-risk searches, and authorize recovery actions.

What makes the command center different from a recruiting dashboard?

A recruiting dashboard displays historical data. The Hiring Command Center displays live operational data — current mandate health scores, active alerts, recovery queue status — and allows immediate action from within the interface.

Can different team members see different views?

Yes. TA leaders see full operational data. CHROs see strategic performance and team health. CEOs see executive summary views with financial impact.

How does the command center handle urgent situations?

When a mandate crosses a critical health threshold, the command center surfaces an alert with the specific trigger signals and a recommended intervention. Authorized users can approve a recovery action directly from the alert.

Is the command center real-time?

Yes. Health scores update continuously based on incoming telemetry signals. Alerts fire within minutes of crossing thresholds. Recovery queue status updates as actions execute.
