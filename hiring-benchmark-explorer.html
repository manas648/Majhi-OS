How to Read These Benchmarks

Market median figures represent the industry average for active searches on the open market — contingency firms, internal TA teams, and general LinkedIn sourcing. They reflect what the typical search looks like, not what is achievable with operational infrastructure.

The stall rate measures searches that have not produced a placed candidate by week 10. This is not the same as searches that fail — many stalled searches eventually close, but they do so with additional cost, team friction, and compounding delay. The 68% baseline for VP searches reflects patterns from Majhi OS mandate analysis and published executive search research.

Offer acceptance rates diverge sharply between contingency and retained models because the vetting process and hiring manager alignment are structurally different. Contingency firms present volume; retained firms present fit.

Industry and stage adjustments are applied as published modifiers based on talent supply and demand dynamics in each segment. They are disclosed in the adjustment note when active.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average time to fill a VP Sales role?

The market median for a VP Sales search is 94 days in 2026. Searches that use a retained model with operational monitoring close in 30–45 days on average.

Why do so many executive searches stall past week 10?

68% of VP searches stall past week 10 because no system is monitoring mandate health in real time. By the time a recruiter flags a problem, the candidate pipeline has already decayed and the hiring manager has disengaged.

What is the difference in offer acceptance between contingency and retained search?

Contingency searches average 59–70% offer acceptance depending on role type. Retained searches achieve 88–93% acceptance. The difference is candidate vetting depth and hiring manager alignment.

How do these benchmarks vary by industry?

AI/ML and Cybersecurity roles run 10–15% longer than the baseline due to severe candidate supply constraints. SaaS and Fintech are near baseline. The adjusted estimates in this tool apply published industry research to baseline role benchmarks.

What does Majhi OS do with benchmark data?

Majhi OS monitors live search performance against these benchmarks in real time via the Hiring Health Score. When a mandate deviates from its expected trajectory, the system flags it before it stalls.
