What Is the Hiring Visibility Framework?

The Hiring Visibility Framework is the observability architecture inside Majhi OS that provides real-time operational visibility into every active mandate. It surfaces the data that CEOs, CHROs, CFOs, and TA leaders need to manage hiring as an operational system — live health scores, trajectory indicators, financial impact metrics, and team performance data.

Four Visibility Dimensions

The framework surfaces visibility across four operational dimensions:

🏥

Mandate Health Visibility

Real-time health scores for every active search — immediately surfacing which mandates are on track, which are at risk, and which need intervention.

👥

Team Performance Visibility

Recruiter utilization, activity patterns, shortlist approval rates, and behavioral signals that indicate capacity or quality issues before they affect outcomes.

📈

Pipeline Velocity Visibility

Funnel conversion rates, stage-by-stage velocity, and pipeline depth indicators that reveal where searches are accelerating or stalling.

💰

Financial Impact Visibility

Cost of open seats, projected time-to-fill, revenue impact of delayed hires — the financial language that makes hiring a CFO-level concern.

The Operational Visibility Gap

Most executive teams cannot answer basic questions about their hiring system: Which mandates are on track? Which recruiters are overloaded? Which searches will miss their close date?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Hiring Visibility Framework?

The Hiring Visibility Framework is the observability architecture inside Majhi OS that provides real-time operational visibility into every active mandate — health scores, pipeline velocity, team performance, and financial impact — for CEOs, CHROs, and TA leaders.

How is real-time visibility different from a status report?

Status reports show what happened last week, require manual data collection, and arrive too late to trigger intervention. Real-time visibility shows what is happening now — allowing leaders to see a mandate trending toward failure and authorize intervention while recovery is still possible.

What does the CFO see in the Hiring Visibility Framework?

Financial impact of open seats, projected cost of current hiring velocity vs target, recruiter team cost per placement, and ROI on the Majhi OS engagement — in the financial language CFOs already use.

What does the CEO see in the Hiring Visibility Framework?

Which mandates are on track for the organizational plan, which roles are at risk of missing their target date, and whether the hiring system has the capacity to execute the growth plan.

Does the Hiring Visibility Framework replace existing reporting?

It replaces weekly status meetings and manual spreadsheet reports. Most clients eliminate 2–3 recurring internal meetings within the first 30 days because the information those meetings provided is now visible in real time.
