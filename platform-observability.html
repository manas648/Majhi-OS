The Signal Layer

Four signal types. One unified health view.

DevOps observability is built on three signal types: logs, metrics, and traces. Majhi OS applies an equivalent framework to hiring operations — with a fourth signal type specific to mandate dynamics.

All four signals feed the Hiring Health Score: a single, continuously updated composite that gives teams instant mandate status.

Logs

Every candidate interaction, outreach touch, stage transition, and decision point — timestamped and queryable. Full operational history for every mandate, every candidate, every action.

Metrics

Pipeline velocity, funnel conversion rates, outreach response decay, recruiter utilisation, SLA proximity — quantified, trended, and monitored against thresholds.

Traces

End-to-end candidate journey from first outreach to offer decision — every stage transition, every delay, every decision point. Traces make bottlenecks visible. Visible bottlenecks get fixed.

Alerts

Threshold-based alerts fire before SLAs breach. Mandate health alert. Reply rate decay alert. Pipeline depletion alert. Recruiter load alert. Each alert has a mapped automated response.

The Health Score

One number. Every mandate. Updated continuously.

The Hiring Health Score is the primary observability output of Majhi OS. It combines pipeline depth, outreach response rate, time-in-stage, recruiter capacity, and SLA proximity into a single composite score per mandate.

Teams don't need to read five dashboards. They need one number — and the ability to drill down when that number moves.

Composite Calculation

The Health Score weights five dimensions: pipeline depth (25%), outreach response rate (25%), time-in-stage vs. SLO (20%), recruiter capacity utilisation (15%), and candidate engagement trend (15%).

Drill-Down

Click on any score to see which dimension is driving it. Is it pipeline depletion? Response rate decay? SLA proximity? The drill-down tells you what the recovery action should be before you ask.

Trend View

Health Score history over the mandate lifecycle. Where did it drop? What recovered it? The trend view makes the connection between actions and outcomes visible — so teams learn what works.
