What Is Recruiting Intelligence?

Recruiting intelligence is the operational knowledge that emerges from systematically analyzing mandate outcomes, candidate behavior, recruiter performance, and market signals across executive searches. It is the difference between a recruiter who knows what happened on their last 20 searches and a system that knows what happens across thousands of similar searches — and can apply that knowledge to your active mandates in real time.

Four Types of Recruiting Intelligence

Majhi OS generates and applies four types of recruiting intelligence across every active mandate:

🔍

Failure Pattern Intelligence

Knowledge of which telemetry patterns reliably predict mandate failure — so the system can alert before failure occurs rather than diagnose after it does.

🎯

Recovery Action Intelligence

Knowledge of which recovery interventions work for which failure patterns — so the system can recommend high-probability actions rather than generic advice.

👥

Recruiter Performance Intelligence

Knowledge of which recruiter behaviors correlate with faster closes and higher shortlist approval — propagated across the team to raise baseline performance.

🌐

Market Signal Intelligence

Knowledge of how market conditions affect search outcomes — compensation benchmarks, candidate availability, competitive search activity — integrated into every strategy recommendation.

Intelligence That Compounds

The most important characteristic of Majhi OS recruiting intelligence is that it compounds. Every mandate adds to the pattern library. Every recovery action adds to the effectiveness database. Every recruiter behavior pattern adds to the performance map.

Intelligence vs. Data: The Operational Difference

Data tells you what happened. Intelligence tells you what it means and what to do. Most recruiting teams have abundant data — ATS records, outreach metrics, time-to-fill reports. What they lack is the operational intelligence layer that interprets that data and converts it into specific, actionable decisions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is recruiting intelligence?

Recruiting intelligence is the operational knowledge that emerges from analyzing mandate outcomes, candidate behavior, recruiter performance, and market signals. It is not data — it is interpreted, actionable knowledge that turns data into specific operational decisions.

How is Majhi OS recruiting intelligence different from ATS analytics?

ATS analytics show historical metrics. Majhi OS recruiting intelligence interprets those signals and converts them into real-time decisions: which mandates to intervene on, which recovery sequences to activate, which recruiter behaviors to adjust.

Does the intelligence improve over time?

Yes — this is the core compounding value proposition. Every mandate adds to the failure pattern library and recovery action effectiveness database.

How does Majhi OS apply recruiting intelligence to active mandates?

The intelligence layer runs continuously across all active mandates — comparing current telemetry patterns to historical failure signatures and generating specific recommendations for each mandate.

Who can access Majhi OS recruiting intelligence?

The intelligence is surfaced to TA leaders and search directors through the Hiring Command Center, and in candidate presentations and executive reporting.
