Mandate Health Score

Response Decay Rate

Shortlist Approval Rate

What percentage of submitted candidates get advanced by the hiring manager. Low approval rates signal brief misalignment, not candidate scarcity. Majhi OS tracks and escalates automatically.

Common Questions

What are the most important talent acquisition metrics?

The most impactful TA metrics are operational, not activity-based: mandate health score (live), response decay rate, shortlist approval rate, pipeline stage conversion, and recruiter load index. These predict outcomes before they occur. Time-to-hire and cost-per-hire are useful for post-mortem analysis but not for real-time management.

How do you measure recruiting team performance?

Recruiting team performance is best measured through mandate-level outcomes: health scores, shortlist quality, time-to-qualified-candidate, and recovery playbook execution. Majhi OS tracks all of these per recruiter and per mandate, creating accountability at the search level rather than in aggregate averages.

What is a talent acquisition operations platform?

A TA ops platform manages the systems, processes, and tools behind the hiring function. Most provide workflow management and reporting. Majhi OS goes further — it monitors mandate health in real time, predicts failures, and executes recovery sequences autonomously without manual orchestration.

Why do recruiting metrics often mislead leadership?

Most recruiting dashboards show lagging averages that smooth over individual mandate failures. A TA team with a 45-day average time-to-fill can still have 30% of searches stalling past 90 days. Majhi OS provides mandate-level visibility so leadership sees the actual distribution, not just the average.
