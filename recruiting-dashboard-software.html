Common Questions

What should recruiting dashboard software show?

A recruiting dashboard should show mandate-level health (not just averages), pipeline stage conversion per search, recruiter load balance, response decay trends, and hiring velocity. Most dashboards stop at aggregate metrics. Majhi OS provides mandate-level intelligence with autonomous alerts.

Why isn't a recruiting dashboard enough?

Dashboards require someone to check them. Most hiring problems develop gradually — response rates drop a little each week, pipelines thin slowly — until the search is in crisis. Majhi OS monitors continuously and acts at the first threshold breach, before the crisis.

What is the Majhi OS Hiring Health Score?

The Hiring Health Score is a composite real-time score for every active mandate — aggregating outreach performance, pipeline velocity, stage conversion, and recruiter load. It's the primary signal Majhi OS uses to determine when to execute recovery sequences. Scores update continuously, not on a weekly report cycle.

How does Majhi OS differ from ATS reporting?

ATS reporting shows what candidates did — what stage they're in, when they applied, whether they advanced. Majhi OS shows what the search itself is doing — whether it's healthy, deteriorating, or in recovery. These are fundamentally different questions requiring fundamentally different systems.
